Final Step: Close the Case


When a case is closed in the real EPIC system, a few important things happen to keep everything transparent and accountable.


First, your Unit Head gets an automatic email that includes:

  • The incident summary
  • The case notes recorded from start to finish
  • Direct links to approve or reject the closure from the email

    • At the same time, the closure request gets added to the Cases to Close queue in EPIC — just like it does now. That queue lets Unit Heads track and manage closure approvals directly from the system.


    In this updated version, the Cases to Close will also highlight and record how long cases have been sitting with Unit Heads awaiting closure. That way, accountability is shared — not just with officers, but also with leaders who approve final outcomes.


    This step helps maintain a clear record of how each case was resolved and makes sure everyone’s in sync before the case officially wraps up.
