About Regulatory Triage


The regulatory triage system starts by scoring the *Likelihood* and *Consequence* of environmental or health risks. These are multiplied to give a **primary risk score**.

If this score is **11 or more**, it indicates a higher level of risk. At this point, additional factors should be considered:

  • How often this type of issue occurs (Frequency)
  • How sensitive the receiving environment is
  • Whether the public is concerned about it
  • The compliance history of the person or business involved
