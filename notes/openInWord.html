Editing in Word

Clicking the Edit in Word button will open a downloadable version of this document in Microsoft Word format. 📄

This allows you to make detailed changes using Word's formatting tools — like adjusting layout, adding signatures, or tweaking legal references — without being limited by the browser interface.

Once edited, the document can be saved, shared, or printed just like any other Word doc. Easy peasy, professional vibes. 🧑‍💼✨
